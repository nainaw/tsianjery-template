# TSM 1.0 (Tsianjery Simple Markup)

TSM is a very simple text format designed for publishing the annual Tsianjery devotional.

Its philosophy is simple:

> The author writes only the devotional content.
> The publishing system takes care of numbering, formatting, navigation and layout.

---

# General Structure

A TSM document contains:

1. Quarter title
2. Quarter subtitle
3. One or more lessons

Example:

TELOVOLANA II

MAMPIFANDRAY NY LANITRA SY NY TANY :
KRISTY AO AMIN'NY KOLOSIANA SY FILIPIANA

================================================

Enjehina, nefa tsy nafoy

Mifalia mandrakariva amin'ny Tompo,
hoy izaho indray mifalia.

Filipiana 4:4

------------------------------------------------

Anton'ny fisaorana sy ny vavaka

Ary matoky indrindra aho...
